About

I am a researcher in mathematics and Bayesian statistics at Centre Borelli (ENS Paris-Saclay). I received my PhD in 2025 from Ecole polytechnique, under the supervision of Josselin Garnier.
My main interests are: Objective bayes, Surrogate modeling, Uncertainty quantification, Sensitivity analysis.

For more, have a look to my research or check my resume. I may also be reachable hitting contact.
